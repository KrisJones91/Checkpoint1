    Sammy Sosa is best known for his batting abilities and was a key player in his 13 years with the team. In 1998, Sosa hit 66 home-runs establishing his dominance while rivaling Mark McGuire for the years home run title.

    Harry Caray was a sportscaster, spending the final 16 years of his career with the Chicago Cubs. Most fans probably remember him for his 7th inning stretch routine, in which he popularized the singing of "Take Me Out to the Ballgame."

    The Legend

    The Cubs were originally known as the "White Stockings" but changed their name in 1903. The team is a founding member of the National League. They hold the modern-era record for games won in a season with 116 games won in 1906 (116-36). Their most recent World Series appearance in 2016, ended in victory, snapping the longest World Series droubt of over 100years.
